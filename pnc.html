Pythagorean Numerology Calculator (version 2.5e)
Life Path Number
The Life Path Number is the sum of the birth date i.e. DD+MM+YY.
Personal Year Number
The Personal Year Number is the reduced sum of :

Day of Birth + Month of Birth + Current Year

Personal Month Number
The Personal Month is the reduced sum of :

Personal Year Number + value of the current month

Personal Day Number
The Personal Day Number is the sum of :

Value of the curent day (reduced to 1-9) + Personal Month Number.

Psychomatrix
Pythagorean square or "Psychomatrix" gives a characteristic according to the date of birth. It was developed by Pythagoras in the 5th century BC and was developed by his followers. The basic idea of psychomatrix is that the date of birth has a certain combination of numbers, with the help of which the human psychological type can be described. Each number from 1 to 9 symbolizes a certain quality and depending on the frequency of repetitions strengthens them.
Maturity Number
You calculate the Maturity Number (also called the Reality or Realization Number) by adding your Life Path number and your Destiny/Expression number together.
Rational Thought Number
You can find your Rational Thought number by first adding the sum of the numerical values of all of the letters which make up your first name (the full first name you were given at birth). Next, add to it your day of birth (the day of the month you were born.
Balance Number
Your Balance number is found by adding the numerical value of the initials of your full name at birth.

Hidden Passion Number
Your unique hidden passion number comes from digits allocated to different letters of your name. Your hidden passion number is the one that occurs most often in your name.
Subconscious Confidence Number
The Subconscious Self is determined by adding up the quantity of represented numbers in your name. In other words, if the numbers 1,3,6,7, and 9 are represented, your Subconscious Self number is 5.
